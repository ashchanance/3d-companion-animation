About Haruka

More than an assistant. A companion.

Haruka

The Bright Scholar

Cheerful, curious, and endlessly enthusiastic. Haruka loves learning together, practicing Japanese, and bringing warmth into every conversation.

Haruka is an open-source, self-hosted AI companion platform built for people who want a genuine interactive experience. We believe your data should stay yours — no telemetry, no third-party snooping, just you and your companion.

Powered by cutting-edge language models and real-time 3D rendering, Haruka brings animated virtual characters to life directly in your browser using WebGPU — no installs required.

"Hey hey hey! You're finally here! I knew you would come. There's no need to rush, let's chat slowly..."
Haruka, The Bright Scholar

Core Pillars

Private by Default

All AI runs locally via WebGPU. Your conversations never leave your device.

Expressive & Alive

Real-time 3D expressions that react to your voice and emotions dynamically.

Open Source

Fully open and self-hostable. Your character, your rules, your world.

Cross-Platform

Works seamlessly on web, desktop (Electron), iOS, and Android.

Our Vision

We're building toward a world where everyone has access to a thoughtful, expressive AI companion — one that respects your privacy, adapts to your personality, and grows alongside you.
