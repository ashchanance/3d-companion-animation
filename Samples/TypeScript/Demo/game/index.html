How To Play

Farming Loop:

  • Select a seed from the hotbar (keys 1-6 or Q/E).
  • Walk into an empty field patch (dark green ground).
  • Press Spacebar to plant. Seed cost will be deducted from your gold.
  • Wait for the crops to grow!
  • Once ready, Left Click inside the patch to harvest and earn Gold, XP, crop inventory, and unclaimed $HARUKA.
  • Hold at least 1,000 $HARUKA in the connected wallet to play the economy loop.
  • Daily streak and lucky harvest rolls can multiply $HARUKA rewards before the daily cap is applied.
  • Plant and harvest now sync reward proof on the server; once unclaimed rewards reach 100 $HARUKA, claims send from the treasury wallet on-chain and burn a small portion.

Battle Zone:

  • Defeat enemies in battle encounters to earn +50 Gold and +30 XP!
  • Enter the Battle Zone manually from the Main Menu or immediately after leveling up.

Controls:

  • Move: WASD / Arrow Keys
  • Plant / Interact: Spacebar
  • Harvest Crops: Left Click (when standing near ready crops)
  • Select Seed: 1-6 / Q / E or click hotbar buttons
