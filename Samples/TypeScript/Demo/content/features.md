## Feature List

### 🤖 AI-Powered Conversation
**Tag:** Stable

Connect to 20+ leading language model providers — OpenAI, Claude, DeepSeek, Gemini, and more. Haruka adapts to your preferred model and maintains rich, context-aware conversations that feel genuinely natural. Switch providers anytime without losing your conversation style.

### 🎤 Real-time Voice
**Tag:** Stable

Natural voice interaction using advanced speech recognition and text-to-speech technologies. Just talk — Haruka listens and responds in real time, no typing needed.

---

### 🎮 Gaming Companion
**Tag:** Beta

Capable of responding and understanding popular video games in real-time. Haruka can react, comment, and strategize alongside you while you play.

---

### 💻 WebGPU Acceleration
**Tag:** Stable

Full support for high-speed, secure, and completely private local AI processing. Haruka runs AI inference directly on your GPU — zero data leaves your device.

---

### ✨ Live 3D Expressions
**Tag:** Stable

Highly expressive 3D models moving dynamically in response to touch and conversational voice. Every interaction triggers real, lifelike animations.

---

### 🌐 20+ LLM Providers
**Tag:** Stable

Connectable to 20+ large language model providers including OpenAI, Claude, and DeepSeek. One companion, unlimited intelligence options.

---

### 🗣️ Multilingual Support
**Tag:** Stable

Native English and Japanese support built in. Practice your Japanese naturally with Haruka — she's patient, encouraging, and always happy to help.

---

### 📱 PWA & Cross-Platform
**Tag:** Beta

Access your companion anywhere with a responsive web interface and native-like app installation. iOS and Android support via Capacitor. Works offline as a PWA.

---

### 💳 x402 Payments
**Tag:** Live on Devnet

HARUKA now supports an x402 payment challenge for developer API traffic, giving autonomous agents a clean pay-per-request path to premium actions, partner APIs, and paid workflows through the HTTP 402 standard.

---

### 🔒 Self-Hostable
**Tag:** Stable

Full control over your setup. Deploy Haruka on your own infrastructure — your data, your servers, your rules. No vendor lock-in, ever.
